How BakaBoost works

Get set up in minutes and start earning from your passion.

1

Create your page

2

Add your BakaBoost link to your social bios, YouTube description, or Discord — anywhere your fans are.

Start earning ♥

Fans send tips, buy from your shop, or join as members. You focus on creating while we handle the rest.
